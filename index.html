About us

Well-being is an non-profit organization that provides physchotherapeutic, financial and wellness support. Our purpose is to help people who are facing difficulties in their life and provide our guidance so as to improve their life quality.

Who are we?

We are a team of experienced professionals from the psychology, economy and healthcare sector dedicated to provide you simple and effective solutions to daily problems.

Our staff has more than 5 years of experience in their fields so as to be able to provide support even to the most challenging cases.

What we do?

Our job is to work together, identify the area of your life you want to improve and set up a plan to make it happen! Each plan focuses on the three pillars of the well-being; Health, Social Connections, Financial Stability.

These pillars are interconnected and often influence each other. Promoting well-being involves balancing and nurturing each of these pillars to create a stable and fulfilling life. Read more.

How we do it?

The first step is to get in touch to arrange a 1-hour welcome call so as to identify your needs.This can be arranged at your convenience at any day between Monday-Saturday at any time between 09:00 a.m to 17:00 p.m.

Once we met we finish our call we are going to set up a specialised 3-person team focused on your needs and in the next 3 hours we will have a weekly schedule based on your lifestyle.

The only thing that you have to do is to get in touch with us is to book your call.
